About Us

“Make it simple, but significant” is the core belief of our design philosophy. It has led us to a dynamic career and has given us opportunities to work with many premium organizations. We have successfully handled residential as well as commercial projects. We believe that design is a journey of discovery and we associate ourselves with a team of skilled craftsmen for bringing our designs to reality.

We seek to attain precision by constantly refining our design ideas, delivering quality and providing economic solutions. After a thorough briefing from our customers, we appreciate our liberty with designing. While planning for every detail conscientiously, customer requirements and preferences remain our top priority. The space is carefully crafted to echo your personality. We also believe in integrating nature with interiors to form a beautiful environment as indoor plants breathe life and freshness into your home and make it feel alive!

Our prime objective is to design a unique space that resonates with you by using materials that suit your budget and minimizing wastage. Our love for arranging things since childhood furthered by our education and our dedicated work in designing keeps us highly motivated and strives towards achieving our objective.

Give Empyrean Spaces an opportunity to build your own heaven on earth.

Our Team

Interior designers team

The foundation of our team is our strong friendship and alignment of our professional interests.

We both completed our education in interior and landscape design together in 2013 from premier institutes like Marathwada Mitra Mandal's School of Interior Design (MMSID) and Jagannath Rathi Vocational Guidance and Training Institute (JRVJTI), Pune. Post our education, we worked towards developing our skills under the able guidance of eminent professionals from the field for more than 4 years individually, before hitting the ground ourselves.

With education and work experience as our support system, our aim is to constantly evolve through hard work and innovation.
